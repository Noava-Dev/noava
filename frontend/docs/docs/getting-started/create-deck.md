# Create Your First Deck

Once you have an account, you can create your first study deck. Decks are where your flashcards live and where you can review content.

## Steps

1. Go to the **Dashboard**.

2. In the sidebar on the left, click **Decks**.

3. Click the **Create Deck** button at the top of the page.

4. Fill in **Deck Details**
   - **Name**: Give your deck a descriptive title.
   - **Description** (optional): Explain what this deck covers.
   - **Language**: Select the main language for your deck.
   - **Cover Image** (optional): Upload a relevant cover image.
   - **Visibility**: Choose whether the deck is private or public.

![Create Deck](/img/getting-started/create-deck.png)

5. Click **Create Deck**. Your new deck will appear in your list of decks and is ready for adding flashcards.
