# Create a Classroom

Classrooms let you organize students and share decks with them.

## Steps

1. Go to **Classrooms** in the sidebar.

2. Click the **Create Classroom** button at the top of the page.

3. Fill in **Classroom Details**:
   - **Name**: Give your classroom a descriptive title.
   - **Description**: Explain what this classroom is for.
   - **Cover Image** (optional): Upload a relevant cover image.

![Create Classroom](/img/getting-started/create-classroom.png)

4. Click **Create Classroom**. Your new classroom will appear in the list.

5. Open your new classroom by clicking on it.

6. Click **Add Deck**.

![Add Deck to Classroom](/img/getting-started/add-deck.png)

7. Select the deck you just created from the list and confirm.

![Deck in Classroom](/img/getting-started/deck-added.png)
