# Create an Account

To start using Noava, you first need an account.

## Steps

1. Go to the [Noava sign-up page](http://localhost:5173/sign-up).
2. Fill in your details:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Email** (use your school email if applicable)
   - **Password**
3. Accept the terms and privacy policy.
4. Click **Continue**.
5. Open your email and enter the verification code.
6. Click **Sign Up**.

After creating an account, you will be automatically logged in and directed to our home page.
