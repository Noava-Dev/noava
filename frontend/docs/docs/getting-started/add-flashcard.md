# Add Flashcards

After creating a deck, the next step is to populate it with flashcards.

## Steps

1. Open the deck you just created from your **Decks** list.

2. Click the **Add Card** button at the top of the deck page.

3. Fill in the **Front Side**:
   - **Front of Card**: The front text of the card.
   - **Image** (optional): Add a relevant image.
   - **Audio** (optional): Upload or record a clip (Max. 15 seconds)
   - **Enable voice assistant**: Enable or disable the voice assistant. This applies to the whole card.

![Create Card Front](/img/getting-started/create-card-front.png)

4. Click on the **Back Side** tab
5. Fill in the **Back Side**:
   - **Back of Card**: The back text of the card.
   - **Memo**: Add any personal notes or reminders for this card.
   - **Image** (optional): dd a relevant image.
   - **Audio** (optional): Upload or record a clip (Max. 15 seconds)

![Create Card Back](/img/getting-started/create-card-back.png)

6. Review the card in the preview at the top
7. Click **Create Card** to save it.
8. Repeat for additional flashcards.

![Deck with Cards](/img/getting-started/created-cards.png)
